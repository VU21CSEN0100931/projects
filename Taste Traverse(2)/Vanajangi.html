Vanajangi

Have you ever watched the sun rising from the midst of dense, wispy clouds? Located near Paderu, Vanjangi Hills is one of the newly discovered hill stations nestled in the verdant expanse of the Eastern Ghats in Andhra Pradesh. Perched at a whopping height of 3,400 feet above sea level, Vanjangi Hills is a scenic destination, fast becoming popular among nature lovers, hikers, trekking enthusiasts, and photographers. As the inky blue colour of the sky gives way to the first rays of the sun, a golden horizon comes into view. It seems as if the sun is rising from a cascade of clouds, blanketing verdant Vanjangi Hills in a gentle embrace. It seems as if God has himself created a canvas full of a riot of colours.
